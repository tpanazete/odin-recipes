Frango com Cerveja

Discover how to easily prepare this delicious Chicken in the pot with beer. Make your day more flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken
  • 1 black beer (33 cl)
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 0.5 dl tomato pulp
  • 0.5 dl olive oil
  • 1 pinch of paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • Lemon juice to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to cook:

  1. To prepare this chicken recipe, start by arranging the chicken, cut it into pieces and pour them into a bowl. Season with lemon juice, black beer, paprika, bay leaf, a sprig of parsley, salt and pepper. Let it marinate for 20 minutes.
  2. Peel and wash the onion and garlic cloves, and chop them finely. Heat the olive oil in a pan, then add the drained chicken pieces and saut√© on both sides until golden brown. Then add the onion and the garlic cloves, and cook a little longer until the onion is golden brown.
  3. Add the tomato pulp and the meat marinade and stew over low heat, adding water little by little, until it is very soft. Remove from the heat and serve decorated to taste. Serve with mashed potatoes.
