Bifinhos de Cebolada

This recipe is so but so good that it can and should be savored from January to December... It always tastes good!

Ingredients:

  • 700 g pork steaks
  • 2 large onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 beer (33 cl)
  • 0.5 dl olive oil
  • 1 cube of broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 pinch of paprika
  • Salt and chili to taste

How to cook:

  1. Arrange the pork steaks and season them with the paprika, bay leaf, beer, salt, and chili. Stir and marinate for 20 minutes.
  2. Peel and wash the onions and garlic cloves and chop them finely. Heat the olive oil in a pressure cooker, add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is soft.
  3. Then add the steaks with the marinade, tomatoes and broth, adjust the seasoning, cover and let the pan boil for 20 minutes. Remove from the heat, let the steam escape and serve decorated to taste. You can serve it with fried potatoes.
