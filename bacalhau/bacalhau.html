Bacalhau com Natas

The cold is already starting to show, so learn how to make this warm fish dish. A codfish recipe that goes in the oven, with mozzarella cheese on top and so many other delicious flavors... How to resist?

Ingredients:

  • 500 g soaked cod
  • 600 g potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 100 g grated mozzarella cheese
  • 400 ml of cream
  • 250 ml of milk
  • 50 ml of olive oil
  • 50 g of butter
  • 4 l egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch flour
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Nutmeg to taste
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

How to cook:

  1. Fry the potatoes, peeled and cut into small cubes, in a pan with plenty of hot oil, then remove them and drain them on absorbent paper.
  2. Heat the milk in a pan and add the codfish. When it is cooked, transfer it to a strainer and set the milk aside. Remove the bones and skin and shred the cod.
  3. Brown the onion and garlic, peeled and chopped, in a pan with the olive oil and bay leaf. Add the butter and flour, stirring constantly, and then add the reserved milk, the egg yolks, and half the cream, little by little and stirring constantly.
  4. Finally, mix the shredded cod and potatoes, season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg, remove from the heat, and remove the bay leaf. Adjust the seasoning and pour into a pyrex or refractory container.
  5. Whip the remaining cream, cover the mixture with it and sprinkle with grated cheese. Bake in the oven, preheated to 180Âºc, until golden brown. Remove and serve.
