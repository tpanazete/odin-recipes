Migas Alentejanas com entrecosto

Do you love pork spare ribs? Check out this recipe for fried ribs with migas à alentejana. In just 45 minutes learn how to make the recipe for fried spare ribs with migas à alentejana, a delicious recipe that everyone at home will love.

Ingredients:

  • 900 g ribs
  • 1 meat sausage
  • 600 g hard Alentejo bread
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 100 g lard
  • 2 tablespoons of paprika
  • Salt to taste

How to cook:

  1. In a bowl, mix the paprika with a little salt and the crushed garlic. Coat the spare ribs with this paste and let it marinate for about 4 hours or overnight.
  2. After the indicated time, put the lard in a pan, let it heat up, and fry the chorizo, previously cut into slices. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the ribs cut into pieces, along with the marinade, and fry until the meat is well cooked. Remove and set aside.
  4. Pour the fat in which you fried the meat into a non-stick frying pan. Add the sliced bread and a little water and stir very well until the bread crumbles into a thick mixture. Let the breadcrumbs fry on both sides so that they acquire a golden crust, and transfer them to a platter. Arrange the spare ribs and the chorizo over the migas and serve immediately.
